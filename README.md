2. Dashboard Overview
<img width="1907" height="967" alt="3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c747b52d-7e88-4560-9f95-56ca91f080f1" />

The Dashboard is the main page of the system and provides a quick overview of your finanical status
Key Features on the Dashboard:

Total Revenue
Displays the total amount earned from all invoices.
Total Invoices
Shows the total number of invoices created.
Active Clients
Displays the number of clients currently in the system.
Pending
Indicates invoices that are not yet paid.
Remaining Balance
Shows the outstanding balance (can appear negative if payments are overdue).
Overdue
Highlights invoices that have passed their due date.
Revenue Trend (Chart)
Visual representation of revenue over time (last 6 months).

👉 This helps users quickly understand their financial performance and take action where needed.

3. About Us
<img width="1887" height="906" alt="4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b35463fc-ef64-4644-a404-0e38d2f19a10" />

Fintrix was created to simplify the way people manage their finances.

We understand how easy it is to lose track of invoices, payments, and client details — especially when things get busy. That’s why Fintrix brings everything together in one place, giving you clarity and control over your financial flow.

With smart invoicing, real-time payment tracking, and an intuitive dashboard, Fintrix helps you stay organized and make better financial decisions.

Our Vision
To grow Fintrix into a scalable solution that supports freelancers, startups, and businesses in managing their finances efficiently.
